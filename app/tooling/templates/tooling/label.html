Printing Instructions:

Please follow these steps to print your label:

  1. Click the "Print Label" button.
  2. Select the "Brother QL-700" printer.
  3. Choose Odd Pages Only
  4. Set the paper size to "Small Address Label".
  5. Set the quality to "300 x 600 dpi".
  6. Choose "Actual Size".
  7. Select "No Margins" (sometimes it defaults to margins).
